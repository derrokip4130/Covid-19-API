Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What is COVID-19?

A1: COVID-19 is a respiratory illness caused by the coronavirus SARS-CoV-2.

Q2: What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

A2: Common symptoms include fever, cough, and shortness of breath.

Q3: How is COVID-19 transmitted?

A3: COVID-19 is primarily spread through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs or sneezes.

Q4: Is there a vaccine for COVID-19?

A4: Yes, several vaccines have been developed and authorized for use against COVID-19.

Q5: Can you get COVID-19 from touching surfaces?

A5: It's possible but less common. The primary mode of transmission is through respiratory droplets.

Q15: Can I travel during the pandemic?

A15: It's recommended to follow travel guidelines provided by health authorities.
